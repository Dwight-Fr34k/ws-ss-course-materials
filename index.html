Course materials: Bram(us) Van Damme — Lecturer: Joris Maervoet

This collection of slides comprises the course materials of the Web scripting — Backend Scripting course, part of the Professional Bachelor ICT study programme, taught at Odisee, Ghent, Belgium.

The materials have originally been developed by Bram(us) Van Damme, who blogs over at bram.us and Twitters as @bramus. These materials are authored by Joris Maervoet in the present fork.

The materials may be used freely, as long as credits to the authors are present and the top right graphical link to ikdoeict.be remains in place.

Suggestions and additions may be mailed to Joris Maervoet, or sent via a pull request on GitHub.
